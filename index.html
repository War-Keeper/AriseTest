Understanding Treatments

Treatments can enhance the appearance and durability of gemstones. We disclose every treatment type to help you make an informed decision about your investment.

  • None: Natural stones with no treatments applied.
  • Heat / No Heat: Thermal processes affecting color or clarity.
  • Minor Oil: Light oiling often used with emeralds to reduce visible inclusions.
  • Diffusion: Surface-level color enhancements.
  • Fracture Filled: Filling fractures with resin or glass to improve appearance.
